Cheese Burger

Breakfast

Level: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup sweet pickle relish
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika

Direction

  • 1. In a small bowl, combine all ingredients and mix well.
  • 2. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
